  3. A 3 -Φ induction motor has a starting torque of 100 % and a maximum torque of 200 % of full load torque. Find the slip at maximum torque?

    Tst = Tf and Tmax = 2Tf . So Tst / Tmax = 2a / a2 + 1 = ½
    ⇒ a2 - 4a + 1 = 0
    ⇒ a = 0.268 ( rejecting higher value).
    Slip at maximum torque a = 0.268 = 26.8 %.
