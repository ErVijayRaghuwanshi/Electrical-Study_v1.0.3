What is Unit Commitment?

This term 'unit commitment' is entirely associated with the economical aspects of power generation companies. Power system does not only deals with power generation, transmission and distribution. It also tries to minimize the cost at every aspect, be it generation, transmission and distribution.

To a huge network there are more than one generating stations are connected, in short these are called units. Now the load of demand of an area varies from time to time, it varies almost in hours. Now the generator units connected with loads are calculated depending upon the highest or peak load demand. But during the off-peak periods the load demand is very less. With the network the power loss and transmission cost are higher, that's why the concept of units to network depending upon the load demand. To calculate this, the load demand is measured over a certain period and after that, specific number of units so chosen that the total system become economical and the unnecessary units are kept switched off which ensures huge saving of fuel costs and unit running costs. So it can be said that the main aim of unit commitment is to minimize the cost and increase profit.
