What are dependent current and voltage source?

The depended current or voltage source is such a source whose output current or voltage depends upon current or voltage of another part of the network. In other wards, the current or voltage dependent source are those, whose output is the function of current or voltage of other part of the network. That means, the output of the dependent source is controlled by current or voltage of other part of the network.

There are mainly four types of dependent source.

1) Voltage controlled voltage source (VCVS)
2) Current controlled voltage source (CCVS)
3) Voltage controlled Current source (VCCS)
4) Current controlled Current source (CCCS)

Depended source are very useful in modeling certain types of electronic circuit.
