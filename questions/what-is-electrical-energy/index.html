What is electrical energy?

In simple words electrical energy can be referred as the energy produced due to the flow of current in the circuit. It can be also said that the electrical potential energy. The electrical energy is converted from mechanical energy or chemical energy. For power plants this electrical energy is produced from mechanical energy by the rotation of the turbines. In electrical batteries, the electrical energy is produced from the chemical substances present in the battery. When the electrical energy is dissipate it is either converted into mechanical force (motor, shaft etc) or into the heat energy or any other forms of energy.
