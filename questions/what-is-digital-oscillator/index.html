What is digital oscillator?

Digitally controlled oscillator / DCO is the name used in analogy with VCO i.e voltage controlled oscillator. Digital controlled oscillators are used in synthesizers. DCO is a hybrid digital / analogue electronic oscillator. DCO sometimes describes about numerical controlled oscillator (NCO). NCOs have many applications like digital converters, digital PLL, radar systems,modulators -demodulators ,etc. VCO had unstable tuning while in DCO these limitation was overcome.
