Discuss about Substation Protection ?

Protection of electrical substations is very vital, because they generally deal with very high voltage power. Therefore suitable safety measurements must be taken as a precaution which will safeguard the operators as well as the costly equipments too. Substation protection includes the protection of the transformer, the protection of the bus bar, protection of line from surge, grounding solution, protection of the system from over voltage and over current. The most costly equipment of any substation is the power transformer, and it is the most vulnerable component also. The oil level of the transformer is monitored constantly and the air fan operation is also controlled according to the situation. Buchholz relay is used to the sense the fault, additionally oil level indicator, thermometer etc are connected with the transformer which measures the oil level, temperature of core constantly and sends the data through the control cables.

Surge arrestor or LA is installed near the transformer which will ground the over current. Several switchgears (such as circuit breaker, isolator) are used to ON and OFF the lines during normal operating conditions as well as under faulty condition too. Instrument transformers are connect with the system which sense the earth fault or phase fault, after a predetermined safe value of voltage or current they sends signal to relay coil. The relay coil is then energized from an external source and trips the line to separate the faulty section from the healthy section.
Earthing transformer or grounding transformer is used and they turned ON all the time, they provides an artificial neutral point for the delta connected lines. All equipments of substation are earthed locally. A connection of thin wire is seen at the topmost area of a substation, generally they are connected from tower to tower. This arrangement protects the substations from several surges, because towers are grounded. In this way substation protection involves the safety of all equipments, and when the substation is ON the operation of several equipments is monitored and synchronization is done from the control panel.
