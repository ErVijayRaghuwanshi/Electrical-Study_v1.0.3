Where wave winding is specially used?

Wave winding is a type of armature winding ,which is typically used in DC electrical machine. In case of wave winding, more conductor is used in the armature series connection as compared to the lap winding.

Addition of any series conductor means addition of resistance with armature circuit.

As, V = IR, so the terminal voltage get increased with the rise of series resistance,when current is constant.

So,when the number of field poles is constant then, a wave wound machine has a larger terminal voltage because of more conductor in series connection.

Wave winding is used for low current and high voltage machines. So, in small machines, where the current-carrying capacity of the armature conductors is not in critical stage, wave winding's are used. Wave winding also helps to achieve suitable voltages in this machines.
