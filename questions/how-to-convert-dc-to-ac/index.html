How to convert DC to AC?

In this article you will get the concept of DC to AC conversion. It is often needed to convert the DC into AC voltage for many applications, but the question is how to convert DC to AC effectively and using a cost effective method. Suppose you have a DC source like battery and a switch. And you are switching the DC supply on and off repeatedly at a very fast rate, actually in proper AC conversion, the switching process is made several times per second. Then the output from the switch will behave like a pulsating AC voltage with some frequency. In DC to AC converters electronic Choppers are used as the fast switching device.

Additionally a coil is pre installed which will induce a current in the opposite direction with respect to the source, because the nature of inductive coils is opposing the change in current flow. An inverter uses the technology of DC to AC converter. It stores the electrical energy in the form of direct current into the storage battery. Thereafter converts it in to alternating current before sending it out to power a device. So these devices is designed to do two purposes that is convert alternating current into direct current (rectification) and convert DC current into AC current (inversion).
