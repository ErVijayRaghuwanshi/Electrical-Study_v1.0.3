Discuss the different methods of excitation for dc machine.

In DC machine current carrying conductors rotate inside static magnetic field. For producing static magnetic field excitation is very essential for dc machines, they can be termed as the most important part of the machine also. A dc machine can be termed as an electro mechanical energy converter which only works in the presence of a field excitation. Now there are two types of excitation present for dc machine.

  1. Separate excitation
  2. Self excitation

Separate excitation - field excitation is only possible by dc supply. So when the field circuit is isolated from the circuit of the main dc machine and is charged by separate dc source and has a different circuit altogether then that type of excitation is called separate excitation. Self excitation– when the supply of the machine is taken from the main circuit of the dc machine and the field circuit is a part of the main machine circuit then that type of excitation can be termed as self excitation.

A self excited dc machine can be divided into

  1. Series excitation – in this case the field is in series with the rotor and the armature current itself acts as the field current.

  2. Shunt excitation- when the field is in the parallel with the armature, then that excitation is called shunt excitation.

  3. Compound excitation- both series and shunt excitation winding is present in the circuit then this is called compound excitation.

Both the advantages of series excitation and shunt excitation is found in compound excitation. Shunt excitation machine are of two types.

i. Short Shunt
ii. Long Shunt
