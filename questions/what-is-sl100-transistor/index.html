what is sl100 transistor?

S stands for "slew" and L for "high power high frequency". SL-100 is a general purpose, medium power NPN transistor that is mostly used as switch in common emitter configuration. Its terminals require a fixed DC voltage to operate in the desired region of its characteristic curves. The transistor terminals require a fixed DC voltage to operate in the desired region of its characteristic curves. This is known as the biasing. For switching applications, SL-100 is biased in such a way that it remains fully on if there is a signal at its base. In the absence of base signal, it gets turned off completely.

The emitter leg of SL-100 is indicated by a protruding edge in the transistor case. The base is nearest to the emitter while collector lies at other extreme of the casing.Its an NPN transistor, generally used as a SWITCH made in common emitter configuration.
