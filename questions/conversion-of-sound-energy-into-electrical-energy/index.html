Conversion of sound energy into electrical energy.

Sound energy can transformed into the electrical energy by energy conversion process.Sound energy is a type of mechanical energy because it is nothing but vibrations.So,we have to convert this mechanical energy into electrical energy by some special device.

A microphone may considered as the special device.

Usually,in microphone a diafram is attached & by the vibration on the diafram a wave is generated & after that it converted into electrical energy.

Thus,sound energy can converted into electrical energy.
