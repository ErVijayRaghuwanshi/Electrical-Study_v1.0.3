What is Lightning Arrestor?

Lightning is the most frequent cause of over voltage on distribution systems. Lightning is a gigantic spark. Lightning arrestor LA acts as safety device to discharge over voltage due to lightning strokes which would otherwise flash over the insulator or puncture the insulator. There are different types of LA which is suitable for different voltages and system. The types are – horn gap arrestor, parallel type oxide film arrestor, thyrite arrestor and auto arrestor. Horn gap arrestor- the simplest form of lightning arrestor is the horn gap type. It is mainly used for lower voltage lines. It consists of two horn shaped metal piece separated by small air gap and is connected in shunt between each conductor and earth.
The gap is set in such a way that a flash over occurs in between them when voltage rises to 200-300% of normal voltage. Choke coils are inserted in this type of arrestor. Parallel type oxide film arrestor – the lead peroxide is made into size of 2.5 mm in diameter and is coated with thin film of litherage. They are placed inside porcelain tube, the length of tube being 4 cm for each kV. Thyrite arrestor – thyrite is a dense inorganic homogenous ceramic compound which changes from an insulator to an excellent conductor when voltage is raised about 3-4.5 times of system voltage. Auto arrestor- this arrestor consists of a stack of porcelain disc made up of special clay and conducting substances. Its initial cost is high is high, maintenance charge is low. It is a multi gap arrestor.
