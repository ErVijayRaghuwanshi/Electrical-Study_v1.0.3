What is trickle charging current of battery?

Actually, trickle charging refers to the charging of a completely charged battery when no load is connected with it. This charging rate should be the rate equal to its self-discharge rate and by this way the battery is remain at its fully charged state.

Consider you have a battery which is already charged fully. Then you connect the battery unit with a source such a way that the current can flow in the direction as it was flowing in the time of charging. But the point is in case of trickle charging any load like resistance or capacitance or inductance should not be connected with the battery ,that is at no load. A tiny current flows from the charger to battery circuit at a rate, equal to the self-discharge rate of the battery. This current is termed as trickle charging current.
