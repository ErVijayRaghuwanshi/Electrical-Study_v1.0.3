What is single phase variac?

Transformers which have small rating are sometimes provided with tap changing facilities. Tap changing facilities are obtained by controlling on load tap changer. In this case tap changing can be done without complicated control involved with on-load-tap-changer. Such devices are sold under the trade name of Variac, Regavolt, Regulac, Powerstat, Auto-stat etc. We will discuss about single phase variac in this article. This variac is supplied with single phase ac supply.

Such devices make use of various circuits. The core of the variac is laminated its shape is circular in nature. The winding is placed on the core with usual precaution taken for insulation. A small portion of each turn is left exposed for the various contact points. A sliding contact with winding is made with a thin carbon brush that covers at least two segments. The position of sliding contact is varied along the exposed surface of the winding with the help of a hand wheel. This helps us to get a desired output. The output voltage can be varied between 0 to 120 percent of input voltage. The short circuit current passes through the brush, the resistance of which is sufficient to limit the current to a safer value. There are both 1-ph variac transformer and 3-ph variac transformer which are supplied from 1 ph and 3-ph source respectively.
