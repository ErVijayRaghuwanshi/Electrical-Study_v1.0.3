What are the uses of electrical motors?

Electrical motors convert electrical energy into mechanical energy. They works on both a.c and d.c supply. The electrical motors can be classified as following:

1. A .C motors which includes :

  1. Three phase induction motor (IM)
  2. Single phase induction motor
  3. Three phase synchronous motor
  4. Special motors

2. D.C motors

The applications / uses of different kinds of electrical motors are tabulated below:

THREE PHASE INDUCTION MOTOR:    Conveyors, crushers, plunger pumps, hoists, cranes, elevators , compressors, blowers, fans, machine tools, centrifugal pumps, reciprocating pumps , etc.

SINGLE PHASE INDUCTION MOTOR:   Domestic applications mainly used in Hair dryers, fans ,washing machine, a.c. fans, dairy machinery, small power tools, small farming equipment, vacuum cleaner, mixers, etc.

 THREE PHASE SYNCHRONOUS MOTOR:    Variable speed applications such as high power and high speed compressors, blowers, induced and draught fans, mainline traction ,servo drives, etc.

  SPECIAL TYPE MOTORS:

  1. Reluctance motors – electric clocks timers, signalling devices, recording instruments and phonographs, etc.
  2. Hysteresis motors-  electric clocks and other timing devices.
  3. Stepper motors- tape drives, electric watches, IC fabrication, printers, floppy disc drives, plotters,robotics , textile industries.
  4. Permanent magnet DC motor PMDC – portable vacuum cleaner, drills, sabre saws, etc.
  5. Servomotors- robotics, radars,computers, machine tools, etc.

 DIRECT CURRENT D.C  MOTORS:    DC series motor is also used in lifts, hoists, fans , etc. Being constant speed motor dc shunt motors are used in lathe machines ,mill machines, conveyors.
