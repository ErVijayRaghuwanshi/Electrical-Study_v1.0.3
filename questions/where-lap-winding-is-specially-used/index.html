Where lap winding is specially used ?

Lap winding is a type of armature winding, which is typically seen in a DC electrical motor or generator. Lap winding carries more current than a wave winding machine. Because it has more parallel paths in the armature.

How?

Lap winding has more parallel path in armature winding that is the conductors are arranged in such a manner that they provides several way to flow the armature current. So, for a constant voltage, current carrying capacity of a lap wound machine is much better than the wave wound machine.

As, V=IR, so in large machines suitable voltages are easily obtained because of they have a large number of armature conductors that is large resistance, but the current carrying capacity is critical. Hence in large machines, lap winding are used to provide the proper current through the armature.
