What are ferrites?

Actually, ferrites are a special type of chemical compounds, whose main components are oxidized iron and ceramic & they have very strong magnetic properties.

They are classified in two categories according to their chemical composition.

• (a) Soft ferrite: This type of ferrites are basically a combination of iron and light weight metals like nickel, aluminium etc., where they are used in electrical transformers and other devices that require the ability for the magnetic field to be easily reversed.

• (b) Hard ferrite: This type of ferrites are a combination of iron and heavy metals like barium, cobalt etc.

There are several applications of ferrite material as they are very cheap. They are used as a core material of inductors, transformers, and electromagnets. Hard ferrite materials are often used as magnetic insulators.
