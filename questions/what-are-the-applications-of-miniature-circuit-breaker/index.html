What are the applications of Miniature Circuit Breaker?

A miniature circuit breaker is a device that switches and protects the lowest common distributed voltage in an electrical system. It is designed to protect conductors and insulations from the damage due to over load and short circuit. There are many advantages and applications are as follows:

  1. There is a quick restoration of supply can not be possible in case of fuse as because fuses have to be rewirable or replaced for restoring the supply. But in the case of MCB, quick restoration is possible by just switching on operation.
  2. It can work automatically,and helps in switching off the electrical circuit during abnormal condition of the network means in over load condition as well as faulty condition.
  3. The switch operating knob comes at its off position during tripping, the faulty zone of the electrical circuit can easily be identified. But in case of fuse, fuse wire should be checked by opening fuse grip or cutout from fuse base, for confirming the blow of fuse wire, which is also a great advantage.
  4. The handling MCB is much more electrically safe than a use of a fuse.
  5. MCB falls in two category - a) residential and b) industrial.
    • Residential MCB are most of the time Plug-In types.
    • Industrial MCB are of three types : bolt-on, plug-in, cable-in/cable-out.
