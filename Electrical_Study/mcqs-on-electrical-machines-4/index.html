  16. The synchronous speed for the seventh space harmonic mmf wave of 8 pole, 50 Hz induction machine will be

    Synchronous speed Ns = 120f / P = 120 × 50 / 8 = 750 rpm. Mmf wave contains space harmonics e.g. fifth and seventh harmonics which corresponds to pole five and seven times that of the fundamental. So seventh harmonic pole rotate forward at speed Ns / 7 = 750 / 7 = 107.14 rpm.
