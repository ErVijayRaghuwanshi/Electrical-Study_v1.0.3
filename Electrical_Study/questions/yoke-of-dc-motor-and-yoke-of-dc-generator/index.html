Yoke of dc motor and yoke of dc generator

Actually, is the outer metal frame of a DC machine. This is used to serve the following purposes:

  1. It provides a strong mechanical support for the poles as well as acts as a protecting cover of the entire machine.
  2. Poles generates magnetic flux & yoke provides the flow path for those magnetic flux.

Thus, yoke is considered as an important constructional element of a DC machine.

In small machines,the yokes are made of cast iron but in large machines yokes are made of cast steel or rolled steel.
