What is thevenin voltage?

Actually, Thevenin's theorem is a powerful circuit analysis tool.By applying the this theorem any circuit consisting of several voltage sources and resistors can be converted into a simple Thevenin's equivalent circuit.

Basically, Thevenin's voltage is the open circuit voltage appears across the open load terminal. To measure the Thevenin's equivalent voltage, remove the load resistance from the circuit & then measure the open circuit voltage appears across the terminals of that load, this open circuit voltage is nothing but Thevenin's equivalent voltage.

Determination of Thevenin's equivalent voltage is an important step as this voltage is required to calculate the branch current.
