What is pole shoe?

Poles are the important constructional feature of a machine. Direct current (D.C) machine consists of three major components :

  1. Magnetic field system
  2. Armature
  3. Commutator and brush gear

Magnetic field systems main objective is to create an uniform magnetic field, within which rotor rotates. It is the static part which produces main magnetic flux. Electromagnets are preferred over permanent magnets. Field magnets consists of four main parts –

  1. Yoke / Frame : A hollow cylinder of cast / rolled steel. It carries the flux produced by the poles. Yoke is not laminated. Yoke serves the purpose of protecting the pole cores and it forms a part of the magnetic circuit.
  2. Pole cores: An even number of pole cores are bolted to the yoke. Pole cores is a circular section which carries coils of insulated wires. It is not usually laminated and made up of cast steel.
  3. Pole shoe : Each pole core has a pole shoe having a curved surface. Pole shoe serves two main purposes – supports the field coils and increases cross sectional area of magnetic circuit thus reducing reluctance of magnetic path.
