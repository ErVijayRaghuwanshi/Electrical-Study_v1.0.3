Characteristics of magnetic field

In electromagnetism, the magnetic field refers to a certain area surrounding a magnet. This imaginary field is constructed by the magnetic lines of force. Characteristics of magnetic field are:

• Magnetic field is considered as a unit dipole that is it will have two poles, namely South and North pole. This field cannot be divided. Imagine, you have a magnet and if you try to break it, then you will get a smaller magnet with the same two poles but the strength will changed.

• Smaller magnetic field is automatically arranged in the desired direction with the influence of a larger magnetic field.

• In any electromagnet, the magnetic field is developed by electric currents. The strength of that magnetic field is depended on the amount of current flowing through the conductor.

• In any magnetic field, unlike poles (that is North pole versus South pole) are attracted by each other and the like poles will always repel each other. This is considered as an important phenomenon of a magnetic field.
