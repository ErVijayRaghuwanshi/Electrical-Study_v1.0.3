What for station service batteries are used ?

Station service batteries plays a very important role in every electrical power station. We,know that,every power station deals with very high voltage(typically in kilo volt) transmission line. So,there should be some protection & security system incorporated with the service station.To control the entire service station some low voltage circuit arrangement is required. It is not possible to control the station with such a high voltage because the controlling is done with service personnel.

The costly components of service station like transformers are controlled & operated by the control room. A DC circuitry is provided along the station to controlling purpose.Here,the source of the DC circuitry is those station service batteries.Those batteries provide the required power to drive the control circuit. Station service batteries also energies the relays, alarm system etc, which is a part of the control.

Almost every power service station uses the station service batteries for their controlling purpose.
