What is static inverter?

Static inverter is a device that converts a dc voltage to a stable ac voltage for use in an uninterrupted power system.

Applications of static inverter are :

  1. Variable frequency power source used as a part of a variable speed drive with induction or synchronous motors.
  2. High efficiency DC power supplies.
  3. Induction heating.
  4. Conversion of DC power to AC power at the terminus of a high voltage DC transmission line.
