What are the uses of ferrite materials

Actually, ferrites are a special type of chemical compounds, whose main components are oxidized iron and ceramic & they have very strong magnetic properties.
They are very cheap so they are widely used in several applications. They are used in:

• Construction of several types of permanent magnets.

• Also used as a core material of inductors, transformers, and electromagnets because their high resistance leads to very low eddy current flow.

• A bar of ferrite can reduce the effect of radio frequency interference, so they are used in such applications where prevention from high frequency noise is necessary.

• In special aircraft or ships, a coating of ferrite material is provided, as it is radar absorbing material.

• Widely used loudspeaker magnets are also ferrite materials.
