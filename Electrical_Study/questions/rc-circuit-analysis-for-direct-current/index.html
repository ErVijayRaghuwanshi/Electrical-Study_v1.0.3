RC Circuit Analysis for direct current

RC circuit means Resistance capacitance circuit. The measurement and analysis is quite easy for direct current. A resistor is a bilateral device which absorbs heat and the absorbed heat appears in the form of heat or light and it is always positive. Whereas capacitor is a bilateral device which is used to store energy in the form of charge separation. A resistor works with both DC and AC whereas capacitor works only with AC. The equation describing the current-voltage relationship in a resistor is V=IR, where V is the voltage across the resistor and I is the current passing through, and R is the resistance of the resistor. The equation describing the current-voltage relationship in a capacitor is I=C dV/dt.This shows that for an instanceous change in voltage to occur, the current should become infinite which is practically impossible. Hence voltage across a capacitor is constant.
