What is two wattmeter method?

There are several techniques for measurement of three phase power. In conventional process, the power of each phase is determined individually and thereafter the total power calculation is carried out, depending upon the connection type. In this method, two watt meter or voltmeter ammeter device is required, which is sufficient to measure the power of three phases R, Y and B. This method is used to measure power of three phase star or delta connected unbalanced system. The connection diagram of two watt meter method is shown in the following diagram. The current coils and pressure coils are arranged in a special manner. The current coils of two watt meters are connected in series with two phases. And the middle phases is termed as the reference phase, because the pressure coil of the watt meters are connected across the middle phase. Here the middle phase is Y phase, so the pressure coil is connected across R and Y phase and B and Y phase respectively.

The instantaneous power measured by W1 is IRNVRY and the VRY is the difference of VRN and VYN. The instantaneous power measured by W2 is IBNVBY and the VBY is the difference of VBN and VYN. By the calculation, the summation of those two watt meter readings gives the total instantaneous power of the system. In this way, by two watt meter method, the total power consumed by a three phase load is determined.
