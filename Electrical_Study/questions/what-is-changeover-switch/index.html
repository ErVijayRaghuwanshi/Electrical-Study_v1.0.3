What is Changeover Switch?

Switch is a device which can break an electrical circuit and is capable of making the current flow or interrupt the current flow from one conductor to another. A switch has two piece called contacts which are often metal. Switch operates in two states- open and close state. If pair of contacts are closed it will allow current flow and in case of open state of contacts no current flows between them. We also use the terms make and break for explaining the closing and opening of contacts respectively. MCB changeover switch finds applications both in industries and domestic purpose.

Changeover switch is used for low voltage distribution circuits, where continuous voltage supply is needed. This type of switch is made by some leading companies like Havells, L & T, ABB, etc. Changeover switch is of different range and specifications.
