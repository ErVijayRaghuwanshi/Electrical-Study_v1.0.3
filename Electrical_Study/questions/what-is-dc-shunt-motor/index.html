What is DC Shunt Motor?

Direct current (DC) motor is of three types namely – dc series motor, dc shunt motor and dc compound motor. The method of classification depends upon the connection of armature (the rotating part of the motor) with field winding. The term shunt means parallel connection so from the name we can conclude that the armature is in parallel to the field winding in this type of motor. Here the field winding is also called as shunt field. Shunt motor essentially consists of stator, rotor and commutator. Supply voltage is dc and the load current is divided into armature current and field current.

So from the figure we can write load current = armature current + shunt field current

IL = Ia + Ish

If the supply voltage is V then Ish = V / Rsh

Voltage equation of dc shunt motor is written as Eb = V - Ia Ra where , Eb is the back emf and Ra is the armature resistance.

DC shunt motor is also referred to as constant sped motor. It also has a very good voltage regulation and it is used in lathe machines, conveyors, etc. This motor does not produce high starting torque. There are three operating characteristics of dc shunt motor – speed- armature current characteristics, torque- armature current characteristic and speed –torque characteristics.
