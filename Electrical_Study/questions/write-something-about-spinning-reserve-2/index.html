Write something about spinning reserve

The term 'Reserve' itself explains that it is concerned with something like extra or in reserved condition. Now the definition of spinning reserve can be given as the difference in between the total amount of power generated and the load demand and losses during transmission. So, it can be said that the spinning reserve is the excess or extra power available in the system. The main aim of introducing spinning reserve into the system is to make power available for any emergency condition such as any fault in the network, so that at any portion in the system the extra power is available and faulty area can be operated without the system being shut down completely. Generally the amount of spinning reserve is determined by a given percentage of the forecasted peak load demand. Besides this the unit commitment problem is also considered and the economic side of power production is also looked into.
