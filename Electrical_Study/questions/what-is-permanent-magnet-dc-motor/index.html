What is permanent magnet dc motor ?

A motor is a electrical device that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy. A motor that runs in direct current is called a d.c motor. A permanent magnet d.c motor is a special type of dc shunt motor. For this motor the poles are made by permanent magnet. This permanent magnets are supported by a cylindrical steel stator. The rotor has a conventional d.c armature, windings, brushes& commutator segment. There are 3 types of permanent magnets used for permanent magnet d.c motors.

These are:-- alnico magnets ,ceramic magnets and rare earth magnet.

Here, the permanent magnet produces a magnetic flux for a permanent magnet dc motor. Then the axial current carrying conductors of the rotor cuts the magnetic flux and produces torque.

The input current increases linearly with the increasing of load torque. The efficiency for this type of motor is high than a wound-field dc motor as there is no field copper loss. The speed of the permanent magnet d.c motor depends on armature current and flux. Low voltage permanent magnet dc motor produces less air noise. The disadvantage for a permanent magnet dc motor is that the permanent magnets can be demagnetized by armature magneto motive force(mmf) which can make the motor inoperative.
