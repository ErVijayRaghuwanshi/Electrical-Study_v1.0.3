What is Tesla Induction Motor ?

Most popular type of A.C motor is induction motor. Three phase induction motor is cheap, robust, efficient and reliable. So induction motor IM has widespread applications in different fields. It is most commonly used for industrial drives. Induction motor is also named as Tesla induction motor. Nikola Tesla made the first induction motor which was operated. Thus the motor is named after this man. Tesla was a Croatian by birth and but started working in America. Induction motor is simple and robust in construction. In 1883 Tesla studied the basic principle of induction motor operation. Later he sold the manufacturing rights of poly phase induction motor to George Westinghouse. According to the Construction, induction motor is of two parts- stator and rotor. Apart from these two brushes and commutator are present. Stator is the static part and armature is the rotor which rotates.

Induction motor rotor is of two types- cage rotor and slip ring or wound rotor. Stator windings are connected to voltage supply. Cage or squirrel cage induction motor is the most accepted motor among all AC motors. Tesla induction motor cannot run at synchronous speed so there is a difference between the actual rotor speed and synchronous speed. This difference is called slip speed. Starter is used for starting of induction motor. Different starting methods of induction motor are- direct on-line starter, star-delta starter and auto transformer starter. Tesla induction motor finds its use in centrifugal pumps, machine tools, compressor, crushers, small hoists, bulldozers, etc. This type of motor has good voltage regulation and have overload capacity.
