Types of DC generator

DC generators are classified in some category according to their field excitation systems.

Mainly they are divided into Two categories:

• Separately excited generator: Here the field coil of the DC generator is excited from a external direct current source.

• Self-excited generator: Here the field coil is energized by the current produced by the generator itself. There are subcategories of self-excited generators according to the arrangement of armature & field:

  1. Series wound: As the name says, the field winding is connected in series with the armature, so the field winding carries the full-load current.
  2. Shunt wound: Here the field winding is connected in parallel with the armature winding & thus it is subjected to full-load voltage.
  3. Compound: It is the combination of series & shunt connection of armature & field winding.
