What is soft magnetic material.

Actually, magnetic materials are classified in two general categories, namely soft magnetic material & hard magnetics material.

An important characteristic of soft magnetic materials is, those materials are easily magnetised and demagnetised. In some other words, we can say that soft magnetic materials can hold their magnetic properties temporarily.

Think about a soft iron core. If you wound some turns of wires on the iron core & apply some potential difference, then you will see that the iron core is converted into a magnet, more specifically electromagnet. But, as soon as you remove the voltage source you will see the magnetic property of the iron core is depleted. This is because, when a electric field is applied to a soft magnetic material, then the tiny atoms of that material get aligned. But, when the source is removed this alignment breaks. That's why, soft magnetic materials are easily magnetized by electric current and demagnetized by removing that electric current.

These magnetic materials are depended on the flow of current. More current means stronger magnetic field. So,we can say that we can regulate the magnetism of the soft magnetic material by the flow of current.

Applications:

Soft magnetic materials are widely used in several sophisticated electronic appliances like an MP3 player, a computer. They are also used as the core material of some transformer, relays or inductors as this type of devices deals with alternating current.
